### When to Take Mock Exams

<Steps>
  <Step title="Diagnostic (Week 1)">
    Take a baseline exam to assess starting point
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mid-Prep (Week 6)">
    Measure progress and adjust study plan
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Final Prep (Week 10)">
    Confirm readiness and identify last-minute focus areas
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Week Before Exam">
    Final confidence-building exam under real conditions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Don't take too many mock exams. They're mentally exhausting and less 
  effective than targeted practice. Aim for one full exam every 2-3 weeks.
</Warning>
