## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the central hub for monitoring your BAR exam preparation progress. It displays real-time metrics, study plans, and performance insights.

## Key Metrics

The dashboard tracks four primary metrics displayed as cards:

### Practice Questions Completed

- **What it shows**: Total number of practice questions you've answered
- **How it's tracked**: Automatically increments with each question answered in practice sessions
- **Why it matters**: Demonstrates your overall engagement and practice volume
- **Goal**: Aim for consistent daily practice to build mastery

### Current Score

- **What it shows**: Your current BAR exam score (set during onboarding)
- **Range**: 260-400
- **How to update**: Use the exam result dialog after your exam date passes
- **Display**: Shows your current performance level

### Target Score

- **What it shows**: Your goal BAR exam score (set during onboarding)
- **Range**: 260-400
- **Additional info**: Shows "X points to go" - the gap between current and target
- **Purpose**: Keeps your goal visible and motivates progress

### Study Streak

- **What it shows**: Consecutive days you've practiced
- **Display format**: "X days"
- **How it's tracked**: Based on your `lastStudyDate` field
- **How to maintain**: Practice at least once per day
- **Why it matters**: Consistency is key to BAR exam success

## Exam Countdown Timer

Located in the top-right of your dashboard:

### Before Exam Date

Displays a live countdown with four segments:

- **Days**: Days remaining until exam
- **Hours**: Hours remaining in current day
- **Minutes**: Minutes remaining in current hour
- **Seconds**: Real-time seconds countdown

The timer updates every second to keep your deadline visible.

### After Exam Date Passes

When your exam date arrives or passes:

1. **Dialog Appears**: Automatic prompt to update your exam status
2. **Exam Result Options**:
   - Passed
   - Failed
   - Prefer not to say
3. **Required Updates**:
   - New exam date (calendar picker)
   - Current score (260-400)
   - Target score (260-400, must be ≥ current score)
4. **Update Button**: Saves your new information

After submitting, the dashboard refreshes with:
- New countdown timer
- Updated scores
- Reset study plan based on new timeline

## Performance Insights

Displays your exam performance across different practice sessions.

### What's Tracked

Each performance insight includes:

- **Exam ID**: Identifier for the practice exam/session
- **Total Questions**: Number of questions in that session
- **Correct Answers**: How many you got right
- **Accuracy Percentage**: (Correct ÷ Total) × 100
- **Time Taken**: Total minutes spent on the exam
- **Timestamp**: When the session occurred

### Visualization

Performance insights are displayed as:

- **Progress bars**: Visual representation of accuracy percentage
- **Percentage score**: Numerical accuracy shown (e.g., "85%")
- **Exam identifier**: Shows which exam or section
- **Sorted by date**: Most recent sessions appear at the top

### Empty State

If you haven't taken any practice exams:
- Message displays: "No performance data available."
- Encourages you to take mock exams or practice sessions

## Practice History

Tracks all your practice sessions over time.

### Session Information

Each practice session shows:

- **Date**: When you practiced (e.g., "Jan 15")
- **Section**: Which subject you practiced (e.g., "Constitutional Law")
- **Question IDs**: All questions you encountered (saved in backend)
- **Responses**: Your answers and correctness (saved in backend)
- **Score**: Percentage correct for that session
- **Time Taken**: Duration in seconds

### Display Format

- Sessions listed in reverse chronological order (most recent first)
- Shows last several sessions on dashboard
- Each row contains:
  - Date (abbreviated format)
  - Subject section name
  - Border separator between entries

### Empty State

Before starting practice sessions:
- Shows "Your progress over the last 0 sessions"
- Encourages you to start practicing

## Understanding Your Data

<Accordion title="How is my study streak calculated?">
  Your study streak counts consecutive days with at least one practice session. It resets to 0 if you skip a day. The streak is based on the `lastStudyDate` field, which updates whenever you complete practice questions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What counts as a practice question?">
  Any question answered in a practice session counts toward your Practice Questions metric. This includes:
  - Question bank practice
  - Mock exam questions
  - Timed practice sections
  
  Bookmarked or viewed-but-not-answered questions don't increment this count.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How often does my dashboard update?">
  - **Study streak**: Updates once per day when you practice
  - **Practice questions**: Updates immediately after each session
  - **Performance insights**: Updates after completing exams/sessions
  - **Countdown timer**: Updates every second in real-time
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I manually adjust my metrics?">
  You can update:
  - Current Score (via exam result dialog)
  - Target Score (via exam result dialog)
  - Exam Date (via exam result dialog)
  
  You cannot manually adjust:
  - Practice Questions count (auto-tracked)
  - Study Streak (auto-calculated)
  - Performance insights (generated from practice data)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where is my practice history stored?">
  All practice history is stored securely in your user profile:
  - Session data includes responses, timestamps, scores
  - Accessible via the Practice History section
  - Used to generate performance insights
  - Retained indefinitely for progress tracking
</Accordion>
