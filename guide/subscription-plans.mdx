## Overview

The LSAT Training Platform offers three subscription tiers to match your preparation needs and budget. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

## Subscription Management with Stripe

### How Payments Work

The platform uses Stripe for secure payment processing:

1. **Secure Checkout**: All payment information is handled by Stripe (PCI-compliant)
2. **No Stored Cards**: The platform never stores your credit card information
3. **Encrypted Transactions**: All payment data is encrypted in transit
4. **International Support**: Accepts cards from most countries

### Subscribing to a Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Plan">
    Navigate to the Subscription page and review the three plan options:

## Payment Configuration

### Backend Setup

The checkout process uses:

- **Stripe API Version**: 2023-10-16
- **Payment Methods**: Credit/debit cards
- **Checkout Mode**: 
  - Weekly plan: `subscription` mode (recurring)
  - Full access: `payment` mode (one-time)

### Environment Variables Required

For the checkout to work, the following must be configured:

- `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY`: Server-side Stripe API key
- `STRIPE_WEEKLY_PRICE_ID`: Price ID for weekly subscription
- `STRIPE_ONE_TIME_PRICE_ID`: Price ID for one-time payment
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_BASE_URL`: Your application's base URL

### Checkout Session Details

When you click subscribe, the backend creates a Stripe session with:

- **Line Items**: Selected plan pricing
- **Quantity**: 1
- **Success URL**: `/subscription/success?session_id={CHECKOUT_SESSION_ID}`
- **Cancel URL**: `/subscription?canceled=true`
- **Client Reference ID**: Your user ID for tracking
- **Metadata**: User ID and plan ID for webhooks

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Can I upgrade from Weekly to Full Access?">
  Yes! Contact support to upgrade. If you've been on the weekly plan, you may receive credit toward the full access price based on your payments.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cancel my weekly subscription?">
  Navigate to your account settings and click "Manage Subscription". This will redirect you to Stripe's customer portal where you can cancel or modify your subscription. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if my payment fails?">
  For weekly subscriptions:
  - Stripe will automatically retry the payment
  - You'll receive email notifications about failed payments
  - After multiple failures, your subscription may be suspended
  - You can update your payment method in the customer portal
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a refund policy?">
  **7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee**: If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days of your subscription, contact support for a full refund. No questions asked.
  
  After 7 days:
  - Weekly subscriptions: No refunds for current period, but you can cancel for future periods
  - Full access: No refunds after 7 days due to immediate access to all content
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
  Stripe supports:
  - Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  - Debit cards with card logos
  - Digital wallets (availability varies by region)
  
  Payment methods vary by country. Stripe will show available options during checkout.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
  Yes, absolutely:
  - All payment data is processed by Stripe (a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified provider)
  - Your credit card information never touches our servers
  - All transactions use 256-bit SSL encryption
  - Stripe is trusted by millions of businesses worldwide
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a receipt for my subscription?">
  Yes! After each payment:
  - Stripe automatically emails you a receipt
  - Access past receipts in the Stripe customer portal
  - View payment history in your account settings
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens when my weekly subscription renews?">
  - Automatic charge occurs every 7 days from your start date
  - Email receipt sent after each successful payment
  - Your access continues uninterrupted
  - You can cancel at any time before the next renewal
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do student discounts exist?">
  Currently, the platform doesn't offer student discounts. However, the weekly plan at $19.99/week is designed to be accessible for students. Contact support if you have special circumstances.
</Accordion>

## Managing Your Subscription

### Accessing Stripe Customer Portal

From your account settings:

1. Click "Manage Subscription"
2. Redirected to secure Stripe portal
3. View subscription status and payment history
4. Update payment method
5. Cancel or modify subscription
6. Download receipts and invoices

### Subscription Status

Your dashboard displays:

- **Current Plan**: Shows active subscription tier
- **Renewal Date**: Next billing date (weekly plans only)
- **Payment Method**: Last 4 digits of card (masked)
- **Subscription Status**: Active, Canceled, Past Due, etc.

## Best Value Analysis

### When to Choose Weekly ($19.99/week)

- Exam is less than 10 weeks away
- Want to try premium features first
- Prefer smaller, recurring payments
- Planning to take exam soon and likely pass

**Cost Examples**:
- 4 weeks: $79.96
- 8 weeks: $159.92
- 12 weeks: $239.88

### When to Choose Full Access ($199)

- Exam is 10+ weeks away
- Want lifetime access
- Planning to retake if needed
- Prefer single payment
- Want to keep access after passing

**Savings**: Breaks even at 10 weeks, saves money beyond that
