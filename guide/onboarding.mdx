## Overview

The onboarding process creates your personalized BAR exam study plan by collecting information about your exam date, goals, and study preferences. This takes approximately 5 minutes to complete.

## The 10-Step Onboarding Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Exam Date">
    Choose your BAR exam date from the calendar picker. This helps the platform calculate how much time you have to prepare and create an appropriate study schedule.
    
    - Only future dates can be selected
    - Your countdown timer starts from this date
    - You can update this later from your dashboard if needed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Target Score">
    Enter your target BAR score (260-400 range).
    
    - Typical passing score: 270-280 (varies by state)
    - Competitive score: 300+
    - This score guides your study plan intensity
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Current Score">
    Provide your current or estimated BAR score (260-400 range).
    
    - If you haven't taken a practice test, estimate based on your current knowledge
    - This creates a baseline for tracking your improvement
    - The gap between current and target scores determines your study plan focus
  </Step>

  <Step title="Weekly Study Hours">
    Select how many hours per week you can dedicate to studying:
    
    - 1-5 hours
    - 6-10 hours
    - 11-15 hours
    - 16-20 hours
    - More than 20 hours
    
    This determines the pace and intensity of your personalized study plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Most Challenging Section">
    Identify which section you find most challenging (select one):
    
    - Constitutional Law
    - Contracts
    - Criminal Law & Procedure
    - Civil Procedure
    - Evidence
    - Real Property
    - Torts
    
    The platform prioritizes this area in your study recommendations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preferred Study Schedule">
    Choose when you prefer to study:
    
    - **Weekdays**: Monday through Friday
    - **Weekends**: Saturday and Sunday
    - **Every day**: Full week coverage
    
    Your daily tasks will be scheduled according to this preference.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Focus Areas">
    Select all subject areas you want to focus on (multiple selections allowed):
    
    - Constitutional Law
    - Contracts
    - Criminal Law and Procedure
    - Evidence
    - Real Property
    - Torts
    - Civil Procedure
    
    These areas receive extra emphasis in your question bank recommendations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="BAR Prep Materials">
    Select what prep materials you have access to:
    
    - Official BAR PrepTests
    - PowerScore Bibles
    - Manhattan Prep
    - Kaplan
    - Princeton Review
    - Multiple Resources
    
    This helps align platform recommendations with your existing study materials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Additional Information">
    Share any additional context (optional):
    
    - Specific challenges you're facing
    - Your learning style preferences
    - Time constraints or considerations
    - Previous test-taking experience
    
    This text is used to further customize your experience.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Submit">
    Review all your information before creating your study plan:
    
    - All collected information is displayed for verification
    - Use the "Back" button to make any changes
    - Click "Create Study Plan" to finalize
    
    After submission, you'll be redirected to your dashboard with a personalized study plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Onboarding

Once you complete the onboarding process:

1. **Study Plan Generation**: Your personalized study plan is created based on your responses
2. **Dashboard Access**: You're redirected to your dashboard with today's tasks
3. **Progress Tracking**: The platform begins tracking your study streak and performance
4. **Countdown Timer**: Your exam countdown timer starts displaying in the dashboard

## Navigation

During onboarding, you can:

- **Navigate Back**: Use the "Back" button to revise previous answers
- **Track Progress**: A visual progress bar shows which step you're on (1-10)
- **View Step Icons**: Each step displays a relevant icon for quick reference

<Accordion title="What happens if I skip onboarding?">
  You cannot access the full platform features without completing onboarding. The onboarding data is essential for creating your personalized study plan and tracking your progress effectively.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change my onboarding answers later?">
  Yes! You can update your exam date, current score, and target score from your dashboard. For other preferences, contact support or re-complete the onboarding process.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is my data used?">
  Your onboarding data is used exclusively to:
  - Generate personalized study plans
  - Recommend appropriate practice questions
  - Track your progress toward your target score
  - Calculate study streaks and performance insights
  
  All data is stored securely and associated with your authenticated user account.
</Accordion>
