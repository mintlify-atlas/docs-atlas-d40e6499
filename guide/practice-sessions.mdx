## Overview

Practice sessions are the core of your BAR exam preparation. The platform provides interactive question practice with immediate feedback, detailed explanations, and progress tracking.

## Starting a Practice Session

### Selecting a Subject

Navigate to the Question Bank and choose from these BAR exam subjects:

- Constitutional Law
- Contracts
- Criminal Law and Procedure
- Civil Procedure
- Evidence
- Real Property
- Torts

Each subject contains comprehensive practice questions with varying difficulty levels.

### Practice Session Interface

Once you start a session, you'll see:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Progress Tracking">
    At the top of your screen:
    
    - **Question Counter**: Shows current question number and total (e.g., "Question 5 of 50")
    - **Subject Badge**: Displays the current subject area
    - **Progress Bar**: Visual indicator of completion percentage
  </Step>

  <Step title="Question Display">
    Each question includes:
    
    - **Question Number**: Clearly labeled for reference
    - **Question Text**: Full question content with legal scenarios
    - **Multiple Choice Options**: Four answer options (A, B, C, D)
    - **Bookmark Button**: Save questions for later review
    - **Exit Button**: Save progress and return to dashboard
  </Step>

  <Step title="Answer Selection">
    To answer a question:
    
    1. Read the question carefully
    2. Select one of the radio button options
    3. Click "Submit Answer" to check your response
    4. The selected option is highlighted before submission
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Answer Feedback System

### Immediate Feedback

After submitting your answer, the system provides instant feedback:

#### Correct Answer
- **Green highlight** on the correct option
- **Green checkmark icon** appears
- Confirmation message: "Correct! Great job! You selected the right answer."

#### Incorrect Answer
- **Green highlight** shows the correct answer
- **Gray highlight** on your selected (incorrect) answer
- **Alert icon** on incorrect selection
- Message displays: "Incorrect - The correct answer was: [Option]"

### Detailed Explanations

After each answer submission:

1. **Show Explanation Button**: Click to reveal detailed reasoning
2. **Explanation Section**: Includes:
   - Legal principles and concepts
   - Why the correct answer is right
   - Why other options are incorrect
3. **AI Suggestion**: Personalized learning tip based on your performance
   - Correct: "Great job! You've mastered this concept."
   - Incorrect: "Consider reviewing the relevant legal principles."

### Question Grid Navigator

At the bottom of each practice session:

- **Paginated Question Grid**: View 10 questions at a time
- **Question Status Indicators**:
  - **Black**: Current question you're on
  - **Gray**: Questions you've answered
  - **White**: Unanswered questions
- **Page Navigation**: Use chevron buttons to navigate between pages
- **Direct Jump**: Click any question number to jump directly to that question

### Bookmarking Questions

Save questions for later review:

1. Click the bookmark icon in the question header
2. Bookmarked questions are saved to your account
3. Access bookmarked questions from your dashboard
4. Review difficult questions later for focused practice

## Session Tracking

The platform automatically tracks:

### During Your Session
- Questions attempted
- Answers selected (correct/incorrect)
- Time spent per question
- Bookmarked questions
- Current progress percentage

### Session Data Saved
When you exit or complete a session, the platform saves:

- **Session ID**: Unique identifier for this practice session
- **Timestamp**: When the session occurred
- **Subject Section**: Which subject you practiced
- **Question IDs**: All questions you encountered
- **Responses**: Your answers and whether they were correct
- **Score**: Percentage of questions answered correctly
- **Total Time**: How long you practiced (in seconds)
- **Bookmarked Questions**: Questions you marked for review

## Completion and Results

### Finishing a Session

When you reach the last question:

1. Submit your final answer
2. Review the explanation
3. Click "Finish" button
4. See your completion summary

### Completion Summary

After finishing all questions:

- **Congratulations Message**: Acknowledges completion
- **Final Score**: Shows questions correct out of total
- **Subject Completed**: Confirms which subject you practiced
- **Action Buttons**:
  - Go to Dashboard
  - Take Another Practice

### Early Exit

Click "Exit" at any time to:

- Save your progress automatically
- Return to dashboard
- All answered questions are recorded
- Session data is saved to your practice history

<Accordion title="What happens to my progress if I close the browser?">
  Your progress is saved when you click "Exit" or complete the session. However, if you close the browser without exiting, your current session data may be lost. Always use the Exit button to ensure your work is saved.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I pause a practice session?">
  Yes! Click the "Exit" button at any time. Your progress is saved, and you can start a new session later. Note that sessions are independent - you won't return to the exact same question, but all your answers are saved to your practice history.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How many questions are in each subject?">
  Each subject contains multiple questions (typically 20-50 per subject area). The exact number varies by subject. You can complete all questions in one session or split them across multiple practice sessions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I retake questions I've already answered?">
  Yes! You can practice the same subject multiple times. Each session is tracked separately in your practice history, allowing you to see improvement over time on the same material.
</Accordion>
